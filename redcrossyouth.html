RED CROSS YOUTH

DOING GOOD BY BEING GOOD

Red Cross Youth is Danmark’s largest humanitarian youth organisation, with volunteer’s taking care of vunerable children and young people. Red Cross Youth, depends on government funding and the number of paying members -equals the amount of money given to them. Our tasked was to create a recruitment campaign, targeting young people who was hard to engage. And we needed 1.200 new members, under 30 years. We used Karma as a central element, when you do good - you’ll recieve good. Every member signing up 8 new members, got a festival ticket. Exchange good for good.
