MILLE DINESEN

A LIFE LIVED IN BALANCE

Mille Dinesen is a Danish actor, who, in her youth, experienced a life full of stress. She changed her lifestyle and focused on creating a more balanced and healthy life, nursing mind and body.
Her organic luxury inside our beauty series is a result of many years of experimenting and tells the story of her own journey towards creating a life in balance. The product is made of pure organic and natural ingredients.
