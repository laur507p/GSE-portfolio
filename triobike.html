TRIO BIKE

CLIMATE-FRIENDLY TRANSPORTATION

TrioBike is a danish cargo bike company, who wanted to challange the way we transport ourselves. Could we make people change behaviour and choose bike over cars?
They needed a rebranding and repositioning of the Trio Bike brand.
We created a platform where we invited people to “Live the Trio Way” in an easy going, vibrant and playful manner.
