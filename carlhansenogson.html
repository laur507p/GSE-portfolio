CARL HANSEN & SØN

CARL HANSEN & SØN 2019

For the 2019 Catalogue we shot the masterpiece collection by Hans Wegner that emmerged from the first 3 weeks of collaboration between Carl Hansen & Søn’s cabinetmakers and Wegner in 1949 as part of telling the story behind the design that went on to become icons of modern Danish design. For the Carl Hansen & Søn furniture books and catalogues 2019 we created a brand narrative around craftmanship and and why materials matter in creating longlasting design pieces.
